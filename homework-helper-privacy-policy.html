Homework Helper+ Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 February 2026

Homework Helper+ Privacy Policy Homework Helper+ is developed and operated by JL Mobile Studios, United Kingdom. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: Email: jlmobilestudios@gmail.com 1. Overview This Privacy Policy explains how Homework Helper+ (“the App”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. The App is designed for parents and students. Children may create accounts and use the App. 2. Information We Collect When you use Homework Helper+, we may collect the following information: Account Information Name Email address Authentication credentials (managed securely via Supabase) Device & Technical Information Device identifiers IP address Crash logs and diagnostic data User Content Conversations and homework-related questions submitted within the App Purchase Information Subscription or lifetime purchase status (processed via Apple In-App Purchases and RevenueCat) We do not receive or store payment card details 3. How We Use Your Information We use collected information to: Provide and operate the App Authenticate users Store and retrieve conversation history Improve performance and reliability Provide AI-generated homework assistance Manage subscriptions and purchases Send push notifications related to homework reminders We do not sell personal data. 4. AI Processing Homework Helper+ uses the Claude AI model via a third-party API provider to generate responses. When you submit a question: The content is securely transmitted to the AI provider AI model training on your data is disabled Conversations are stored in our database (Supabase) until deleted You may delete individual conversations or your account at any time. 5. Data Storage & Retention Account and conversation data are stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Data is retained until: The user deletes a conversation The user deletes their account A valid deletion request is submitted via email When an account is deleted, associated personal data is permanently removed from active systems. 6. Children’s Privacy Homework Helper+ may be used by children. We collect only the information necessary to operate the App, such as name, email address, and user-submitted content. We: Do not display behavioural advertising Do not sell children's data Do not knowingly use children’s data for profiling or marketing Parents or guardians may request access to or deletion of their child’s information by contacting us at: jlmobilestudios@gmail.com If we learn that we have collected information in violation of applicable child protection laws, we will take appropriate steps to delete it. 7. Legal Basis (UK & EU Users) Under UK GDPR, we process personal data based on: Contractual necessity (to provide the App) Legitimate interests (to maintain and improve the service) Legal compliance (where required) You have the right to: Access your personal data Request correction Request deletion Restrict or object to processing Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) 8. Data Sharing We share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the App: Supabase (authentication & database infrastructure) Claude API provider (AI processing) Apple (in-app purchases) RevenueCat (subscription management) These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable law. We do not sell or rent personal data. 9. Push Notifications We may send push notifications related to homework reminders or app functionality. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time. 10. Security We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. 11. Fair Usage Access to AI-powered features is subject to reasonable and fair usage limits to ensure service availability for all users. Excessive or abusive use may result in temporary limitations. 12. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
